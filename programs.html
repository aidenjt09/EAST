Questions

Common questions.

Do I need prior club experience to join? +

No — especially for Foundations. We accept swimmers at all levels. Your free evaluation swim helps us place your athlete in the right group for their skill and experience.

What does the monthly fee include? +

Monthly dues cover all practices for your group, coaching, and club entry fees for regularly scheduled team meets. USA Swimming registration (~$75/year) is a separate fee paid directly to USA Swimming.

How does the evaluation swim work? +

We invite your swimmer to join one of our regular practices at no cost. A coach observes their technique and endurance, then recommends which training group is the best fit. There's zero pressure — it's just a chance for everyone to meet.

Can my swimmer move between groups? +

Yes. Coaches evaluate each athlete periodically. When they're ready to move up, we'll have that conversation with you and your swimmer — it's a collaborative decision.

Not sure which program fits?

Register for a free evaluation swim and we'll figure it out together.
